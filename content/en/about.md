Hello! I’m Doug — and ever since my first encounter with a computer, one question has never left my mind: **how does all of this actually work?**

That curiosity sparked a lifelong journey to uncover the mystery. I started with basic computer courses, then moved on to computer maintenance, network technician training, and eventually earned a degree in Computer Engineering.

Along the way, I’ve had the chance to witness the evolution of many technologies — worked with some as they grew, and watched others fade into obscurity. Over the past 12 years, I’ve learned a bit about many different areas, but one of them has always fascinated me more than the rest: **software**. How is it born? How does it grow? How does it evolve? How is it created?

And that’s exactly what led me to create this blog. Here, I want to share not only the knowledge I’ve gained throughout this journey, but also the everyday discoveries and lessons that continue to inspire me in the world of software development.

If you share this same curiosity and passion for understanding the secrets behind technology, you’re in the right place. **Let’s explore this world together.**
